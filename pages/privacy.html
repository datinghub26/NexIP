🔒 Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

This Privacy Policy explains how NexIP collects, uses and protects information when you use our website and online tools.

Information We Collect

When you use NexIP, some information may be processed to provide our services. Depending on the tool you use, this may include your public IP address, browser information, device type, operating system, language, screen resolution and similar technical data.

This information is used only to display results and improve the functionality of our website.


Cookies

NexIP may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience, remember preferences and help us understand how visitors use our website.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, although some website features may not function correctly.


Analytics

We may use analytics services to understand website traffic and improve our content and tools. These services may collect anonymous usage information such as pages visited, browser type and approximate location.


Google AdSense

NexIP may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense or other advertising partners. These services may use cookies to deliver advertisements based on your interests and previous visits to this or other websites.

Google may use the DoubleClick cookie to serve personalized advertisements. You can learn more about Google's advertising practices and manage your preferences through Google's Ads Settings.


Third-Party Services

Some NexIP tools rely on trusted third-party APIs to provide information such as IP geolocation, network details and related services. Requests sent to these services are subject to their own privacy policies.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services whose data is displayed by our tools.


Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect our website and users from unauthorized access, misuse and data loss. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.


Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable laws, you may have the right to request access to, correction of or deletion of certain information related to your use of our services.

If you have questions about your privacy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us using the information provided on our Contact page.


External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. NexIP is not responsible for the privacy practices, content or security of external websites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any sites they visit.


Children's Privacy

NexIP is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information to our website, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

NexIP may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements or technology. Any updates will be published on this page together with the revised effective date.


Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or our data handling practices, please contact us using the information available on our Contact page.
